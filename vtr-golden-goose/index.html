GOLDEN GOOSE

About: Golden Goose Investment Group is a trusted financial institution with over a decade oj experience in the investment advisory industry and portfolio management services. The Group offers innovative yet high-yielding instruments that enable the growth of investor capital and provide financial security and stability

Our History: Over a decade ago, an independent consortium with Royal Family of Ajman joined forces and founded what today is called the Golden Goose Investment Group. Golden Goose's products have been generating passive income for its investors ranging from 6% per annum onwards.

BACK
BULLION SERVICES
We are oriented to the requirements of the market an d are continuously supplied by our valued business partners.
DEALING SERVICES
We assist our clients in pricing all of our precious metals services with trading experts via the dedicated dealing phone line. And we provide our clients with up-to-date market information on a daily basis through state-of-the-art technical analysis.
REFINING SERVICES
We have a unique refinery to provide our customers with analytical and refining services. Our refining partner has state-of-the-art facilities in the United Arab Emirates and an experienced team. We are on the path to excellence and milestones.
HOW WE DO IT
Golden Goose Investment Group enjoys successful cooperation, partnerships, and integration with various regulated entities across multiple jurisdictions throughout the world, allowing them to structure world-class products catering to diverse investors.
CLIENT FICUS
Over the years, Golden Goose has helped its investors become less dependent on revolving debt, enabling them to save for uncertain and unforeseen future needs.
